Premium Chicken

In the Videogame Dark Cloud 2 Premium Chicken is a type of item that restores all your HP, it's got the Premium for that.

The description reads "Fully restores health when eaten. "

For you to make said recipe you'll have to first invent it!

Ideas

  1. Firstly you'll need a picture of a Burning Dragon Fire
  2. Secondly, go take a picture of Pork Dumpling
  3. And lastly one of Special Peking Duck

Ingredients

  • x10 Life Elements
  • x10 Fire Element
  • x2 Super Hot Elements
