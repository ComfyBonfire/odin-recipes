Stamina Drink

In the Videogame Dark Cloud 2 Stamina Drink is a type of item that grants Pumped Up condition, it's a simple attack boost.

The description reads "Bestows energy. A temporary ability boost."

For you to make said recipe you'll have to first invent it!

Ideas

  1. Firstly you'll need a picture of a Bottle
  2. Secondly, go take a picture of Chinese Lantern
  3. And lastly one of the great King Mardan

Ingredients

  • x2 Tasty Water
  • x30 Super Hot Pepper
  • x5 Mighty Healing
