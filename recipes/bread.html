Bread

In the Videogame Dark Cloud 2 Bread is a type of item that restores 32HP, just like in real life. I <3 bread!

The description reads "Restores a little health when eaten."

For you to make said recipe you'll have to first invent it!

Ideas

  1. Firstly you'll need a picture of a Fireplace
  2. Secondly, go take a picture of Polly's Bakery Sign
  3. And lastly one of Wheat Flour

Ingredients

  • x3 Flour
  • x1 Water Element
  • x1 Fire Element
